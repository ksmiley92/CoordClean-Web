Coordly

GIS Coordinates simplified, by SmileyGeo


Convert coordinates between decimal degrees and degrees/minutes/seconds, with a map preview.

Coordly Quick Help Guide

  1. Upload your CSV or XLSX file. The app will detect columns called "Latitude" and "Longitude", "Lat" and "Long", or "Y" and "X".
  2. Select the output format
  3. Click the Convert button
  4. Download the cleaned CSV file
